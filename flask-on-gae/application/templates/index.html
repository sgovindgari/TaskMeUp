Green Cents

Not only are walking and biking great exercise, they are also cheaper. Save money on: gas, car maintenance, parking, and bus/train fares

Take the bus or train, and let someone else drive for you! You can ignore the traffic: sit back and relax, sleep, or catch up on work.

Buses or trains use less fuel per person than automobiles. Thus, public transit or carpooling significantly reduces your carbon emissions.

Trains are unaffected by traffic on the roads. As a result, traveling by train can often be faster than driving, especially during rush hour.
